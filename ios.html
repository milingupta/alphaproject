Requirements

AlphaProject requires iPhone or iPad running iOS/iPadOS 18 or later. It is not available for Mac.

Updating the App

Updates to AlphaProject are delivered through the App Store. If automatic updates are enabled on your device, the latest version will install on its own. Otherwise, open the App Store, go to your account, and tap Update next to AlphaProject.
